Physics is coole

This page can render LaTeX. Inline math like $E = mc^2$ goes inside single dollar signs.

Display equations go inside double dollar signs:

$$\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} \, dx = \sqrt{\pi}$$

More examples

A typical physics Lagrangian:

$$\mathcal{L} = \frac{1}{2} m \dot{x}^2 - V(x)$$

A tensor equation:

$$G_{\mu\nu} + \Lambda g_{\mu\nu} = \frac{8\pi G}{c^4} T_{\mu\nu}$$
